Frequently asked questions

How do live video calls work?

Once you join Pro, youll be able to book 2 live sessions per week with native-speaking teachers inside Talkee. No external apps needed.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade from Basic to Pro anytime directly from your account settings. It takes just a few clicks.

Is there a certificate after completing a course?

Yes. When you finish your course, youll receive a personalized digital certificate that you can download or share.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your plan anytime. Your access will stay active until the end of your billing period.

What if I miss a live session?

No problem you can reschedule within your week or catch up with extra practice materials available inside Talkee.

How long do I have access to the lessons?

Youll have full access to recorded lessons as long as your subscription is active. Yearly plans come with bonus offline downloads.
